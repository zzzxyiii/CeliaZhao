Innovative Design of Multi-functional Instant Hot Water Dispenser

Product Design Integrated design

The design of team water purification, and water dispenser modular combination, convenitent for users for tea, coffee, and other drinks demand.

查看大图

Project Overview

The user can first put the 3 heavy tea drinks he often drinks into the top tea box, when the user wants to drink the drink by controlling the 3 numbers on the screen to choose, and then the tea box will drop the tea to the brewing mouth according to the choice.

It can also expand the human-computer interaction mode through APP intelligent linkage, and the control screen is equipped with common functions at the bottom bar, connect to the APP, support custom water mode, and adjust the cup amount.
